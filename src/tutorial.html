Gestures

Index and Thumb navigation:

Extend your index finger and thumb to navigate on your screen.

Left Click and Double Click:

To execute a standard click, tap your thumb and middle finger; hold to double tap.

Right Click:

For a right-click, tap the thumb to the middle of the index finger while keeping the index finger extended.

Speech to Text:

To execute a speech to text command for seamless typing, do the following gesture.

Scroll Up:

Fold your index and middle finger to execute the scroll-up gesture.

Scroll Down:

Straighten your fingers to perform the scroll-down motion.
