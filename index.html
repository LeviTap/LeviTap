LeviTap - Hand Gesture Control

Introduction

Welcome to LeviTap, a program that empowers you to navigate your computer effortlessly using hand gestures as well as type using voice! This innovative solution allows you to control your cursor with simple and intuitive movements, enhancing your computing experience. Below is a comprehensive guide to using the program effectively.

Getting Started

  1. Download and install HandGestureControl from our official website.
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions for a seamless setup.

Gestures

  • Index and Thumb navigation:
    • Extend your index finger and thumb to navigate on your screen.
    • Fold your index and middle finger to execute the scroll-up gesture.
    • Straighten your fingers to perform the scroll-down motion.
  • Tap for Click:
    • To execute a standard click, tap your thumb and middle finger; hold to double tap.
    • For a right-click, tap the thumb to the middle of the index finger while keeping the index finger extended.
  • Text to Speech
    • To execute a text to speech command for seamless typing click, hold the middle, ring and little finger up.
    • For a right-click, tap the thumb to the middle of the index finger while keeping the index finger extended.

    • Tips for Precision

    • Maintain a comfortable distance from your camera for accurate gesture recognition.
    • Practice fluid and deliberate hand movements for optimal control.
