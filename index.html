Introduction

Welcome to LeviTap, a program that empowers you to navigate your computer effortlessly using hand gestures as well as use your voice to type! This innovative solution allows you to control your cursor with simple and intuitive movements, enhancing your computing experience. Below is a comprehensive guide to using the program effectively.

Getting Started

  1. Ensure you have Python installed.
  2. Then download the requirements.txt and run the following command on the command line / terminal.
    $pip3 install -r requirements.txt
  3. Download and install the program linked below.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions for a seamless setup.

Gestures

  • Index and Thumb navigation:
    • Extend your index finger and thumb to navigate on your screen.
  • Left Click and Double Click:
    • To execute a standard click, tap your thumb and middle finger; hold to double tap.
    Right Click:
    • For a right-click, tap the thumb to the middle of the index finger while keeping the index finger extended.
    Speech to Text
    • To execute a speech to text command for seamless typing, do the following gesture.
    Scroll Up:
    • Fold your index and middle finger to execute the scroll-up gesture.
    Scroll Down:
    • Straighten your fingers to perform the scroll-down motion.

Tips for Precision

  • Maintain a comfortable distance from your camera for accurate gesture recognition.
  • Practice fluid and deliberate hand movements for optimal control.
